Who am I?

Your average, everyday Computer Science student, I initially thought coding would be a piece of cake. I mean, I already spend half my life staring at a screen, right? How hard could it be? Well, let’s just say I was a little naive. The steep learning curve and the endless stream of bugs have certainly humbled me. There have been nights where I’ve questioned my life choices, but then I’ll finally conquer a particularly pesky bug at 3 AM and feel like a coding wizard. It’s a love-hate relationship, really. And let’s be honest, the promise of a decent paycheck doesn’t hurt either.
